  • 株式会社Ridge-i（パートタイム 深層学習エンジニア）

    2024年8月1日 - 2025年2月28日

    北海道大学動物医療センターとの共同研究プロジェクトにアサインされました。本プロジェクトでは、動物のX線画像から骨を除去する画像処理AIモデルの改良と実装を担当しました。通常、人間のX線画像における骨除去には、"骨あり・骨なし"のペア画像が必要とされますが、動物医療においては診療現場で骨のみを除去した画像を取得することが困難であるため、学習用の正解データが得られないという課題があります。この制約に対応するため、他の医療画像モダリティとX線画像を融合し、教師データが欠如している状況でも機能する骨除去AIモデルの設計と実装に取り組みました。この研究は、獣医師の診断支援を目的とした臨床的意義に加え、画像処理と機械学習の研究としても高い挑戦性を含んでいます。

    関連論文： T. Hashimoto, A. Maruo, S. Sekiguchi, Y. Ushiku and G. Shimbo, "Unsupervised Domain Adaptation for Human and Animal Chest X-Ray Bone Suppression," 2024 IEEE International Symposium on Biomedical Imaging (ISBI), Athens, Greece, 2024, pp. 1-5, doi: 10.1109/ISBI56570.2024.10635259.

  • Ridge-i Inc. (AI Engineer Intern)

    August 2024 - February 2025

    Assigned to a collaborative research project with Hokkaido University Veterinary Medical Center. I was responsible for improving and implementing an AI model for bone removal from animal X-ray images. While bone removal in human X-ray images typically requires paired images ("with bone" and "without bone"), veterinary medicine faces the challenge that obtaining bone-removed images in clinical settings is difficult, making it impossible to acquire ground truth data for training. To address this constraint, I worked on designing and implementing a bone removal AI model that fuses other medical imaging modalities with X-ray images and functions even in situations where supervisory data is lacking. This research has high clinical significance for supporting veterinary diagnosis and presents significant challenges in image processing and machine learning research.

    Related Publication: T. Hashimoto, A. Maruo, S. Sekiguchi, Y. Ushiku and G. Projects

  • Bonbi Beergame - Supply Chain Management Simulation

    Enhanced version of the classic Beer Game with original disruption elements added for supply chain management education. I designed and implemented realistic disaster events and market fluctuations (15% probability) not found in traditional Beer Game simulations, extending the learning experience to include decision-making under uncertainty and risk management. Supports up to 4 players in real-time, with roles as retailer, wholesaler, distributor, and factory. Implemented with Flask + Socket.IO for real-time synchronization, Chart.js for performance visualization, and AI players for solo mode. Players aim to minimize total supply chain costs over a 52-week simulation.
